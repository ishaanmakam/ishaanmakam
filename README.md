## What I'm Building
 
### 🗑️ TerraCan — AI Waste Sorting System
Real-time computer vision system that automatically classifies household waste into recycling, compost, and landfill. Trained a TensorFlow/Keras CNN from scratch, iterated through 4 hardware versions to get from 60% → 89% accuracy, and designed a full iOS app in Swift with Bluetooth integration.
 
**Won the $15,000 T-Mobile Changemaker Grand Prize.**

### 🚀 Camp Liftoff — Nonprofit Aerospace Camp
Founded a 501(c)(3) nonprofit that teaches rocketry, CAD, and flight dynamics to middle schoolers from underserved communities. Grew from 10 to 60+ students across two campuses, raised $10,000+ from Blue Origin and T-Mobile, and launched a second chapter at Tyee Middle School whose team qualified for TARC Nationals 2026.
- 📄 [Camp Liftoff Website](campliftoff.org)

### 🛡️ Network Automation & Cybersecurity Tools

### 📡 Noise Contour Modeling — GIS + Python
Modeled airport noise exposure by combining source-level assumptions, terrain elevation, and land use data. Built geospatial sound maps in Python + QGIS and validated outputs against FAA Part 150 reference data.

---
### 🌊 Bio-Inspired Rocket Aerodynamics — Published Research
Investigated whether surface textures from shark skin and humpback whale fins could reduce aerodynamic drag on model rockets. Ran CFD simulations in ANSYS Fluent and achieved 8–12% drag reduction at subsonic speeds.
 
Published as sole author in the Journal of High School Science (19% acceptance rate). Placed 3rd out of 191 teams at MIT IEEE Undergraduate Research Conference — the only high schooler in the top placements.
- 📄 [Research Paper](https://doi.org/10.64336/001c.144090)
